First General Meeting


When:

9/17/2018

1pm-2pm

Where:

SCI 164

Come join us for our first general metting of the Fall '18 semester! Help us kick off the new school year with information about topics such as:
  • Upcoming IEEE seminars
  • Discounted t-shirt sales
  • Micromouse
  • IEEExtreme 24 hour coding challenge
  • Membership
  • Ways you can contribute
  • And much more!
And of course free pizza will be available as our thank you to everyone coming out to support the San Francisco State University IEEE chapter.

We hope to see both new and old members as we welcome in the new semester!
